Bicol is a region in the Philippines that is known for its natural and cultural attractions.Some of the top tourist spots in Bicol:

  • Mayon Volcano, an active volcano with a perfect cone shape that can be viewed from Cagsawa Ruins Park or Ligñon Hill Nature Park.
  • Caramoan Island,Caramoan’s host of secret beaches remain unknown to most tourists, making this isolated group of small islands a distinct castaway paradise.
  • Kawa-kawa features the Passion of Christ - or stations of the cross where you walk up the hills to reach each station.
